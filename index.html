STARSHOOTER

You have stolen a top of the line new starship from your (former) employers at the Vega Private Military Corporation. They have deployed their infinite supply of Class B1304 Starships to shoot you down. Shoot the starships down before they shoot you, or worse, crash into you. For every 25 starships you shoot, your ships automatically drains their power sources to self-heal.

Use WASD or arrow keys to move,
Space to shoot, and esc or P to pause.
